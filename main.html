Air Force Chain of Command SECWAR → Divisions & Teams

Flow of authority and rank from the Secretary of War down through SECAF, CSAF, officer and enlisted chains, departments and teams.

Blue cards = command / category Dark cards = rank, department, or team
  • [HQ] Secretary of War (SECWAR) - BLOXYMORON Overall military policy and top of this branch.
    • [HQ] Secretary of Air Force (SECAF) - V3GE Service Secretary Leads the Air Force; dual-hatted as Federal Aviation Administrator (civil + military airspace).
      • [HQ] Chief of Staff of the Air Force (CSAF) - Ray_blackhawk Senior Uniformed Officer Commands the Air Force on behalf of SECAF and oversees all ranks, departments, and teams.
        • Officer Chain of Command Command progression; colonels and below hold direct control of departments and divisions.
          • General of the Air Force (5-Star) Highest operational commander under CSAF.
            • General (Gen) Leads major joint or theater commands.
              • Lieutenant General (LtGen) Leads numbered major commands.
                • Major General (MajGen) Deputy major commands and large formations.
                  • Brigadier General (BrigGen) Assistant commanders and special directorates.
                    • Colonel (Col) Wing, department, and major division commanders.
                      • Colonel-Level Departments Core organizational units; divisions and teams hang from these.
                        • COAFD — Commissioned Officers AF Department Commander: Colonel Holds and manages the officer corps (2dLt+).

                            Focus: research projects, scientists, officer scientists.

                            Targeting Officer / Analyst, Intelligence Officer / Analyst, Operations Intelligence Specialist, Geospatial Intelligence Analyst, Cryptologic Language Analyst, Airborne Cryptologic Linguist.
                        • MAFD — Main Air Force Department Commander: Lieutenant Colonel Core combat and operational units; minimum rank AB+.
                          • Combat & Security Team Join: A1C+ | Command: Captain (Capt)+
                            Security Specialist, Military Police, Combat Information Specialist, Tactical Air Control Party Specialist.
                          • Operations Team Join: A1C+ | Command: Colonel (Col)
                            Operations Analyst, Operations Support Officer, Strategic Planner, Strategic Communications Officer, Protocol Officer.
                          • Pilot Operations Join: A1C+ (trainees / support roles)
                            Command: Lieutenant Colonel (LtCol)
                            All pilot and flight-line leadership.

                    • Lieutenant Colonel (LtCol) Group and large-squadron commanders; often lead divisions and major teams.
                    • Major (Maj) Squadron / division commanders; many specialist teams are commanded at this level.
                  • Captain (Capt) Company/flight commanders and team leads (MP, Recruitment, Combat & Security, etc.).
                    • First Lieutenant (1stLt) Deputies and officers-in-charge inside departments and teams.
                      • Second Lieutenant (2dLt) Entry-level officers; lead small elements and sections.
                        • Officer In Training (OIT) Officer candidates; on completion they commission as 2dLt.
        • Enlisted Chain of Command Progression for enlisted personnel; most specialist teams open at A1C+.
          • Chief Master Sergeant of the Air Force (CMSAF) Senior enlisted advisor to CSAF and SECAF; top of the enlisted structure.
            • Command Chief Master Sergeant (CoCMSgt) Senior enlisted for major commands and large wings.
              • Chief Master Sergeant (CMSgt) Base / group senior enlisted leaders.
                • Senior Master Sergeant (SMSgt) Superintendents and top NCOs.
                  • First Sergeant (1Sgt) Unit senior enlisted advisor.
                    • Master Sergeant (MSgt) Flight chiefs and NCOICs.
                      • Technical Sergeant (TSgt) Section NCOs and trainers.
                        • Staff Sergeant (SSgt) First-line supervisors.
                          • Senior Airman (SrA) Senior enlisted operators and specialists.
                            • Airman First Class (A1C) Fully trained airmen; minimum for most teams (Research, Cyber, ATC, etc.).
                              • Airman (AM) Junior enlisted.
                                • Airman Basic (AB) Entry-level recruit.
                                  • VET (Veteran) Honorary status for former personnel; no further promotion.
